Where messages go

The form can route your message to the right inbox automatically.

  • General questions go to `info@nzbarr.com`
  • Questions and support go to `hello@nzbarr.com`
  • Setup and troubleshooting go to `support@nzbarr.com`

Tip

For support, include your NZBarr version, platform, and a short description of the issue.
